About ANDURIL.WTF

ANDURIL.WTF provides irreverent, informed analysis of defense technology and the companies building it. The name is borrowed. The .wtf is the editorial position.

Defense technology analysis from the perspective of someone who sits in DIA-INSA briefings and then writes about it on a domain that ends in .wtf. That is not a contradiction. It is the only honest posture for covering an industry that spends trillions while maintaining that public oversight is a security risk.

Coverage Areas:

  • Autonomous Systems — Drone swarms, autonomous weapons, lethal autonomous weapons systems (LAWS), and the ethics of machines that make kill decisions. The OODA loop at machine speed with no human in it.
  • Military AI — AI integration into military decision-making, intelligence analysis, and operational planning. DIA's vision of "intelligence at machine speed with human tradecraft." What that looks like when it works. What it looks like when it does not.
  • Defense Procurement — How the defense acquisition system actually works (slowly, expensively, with lobbying). OTA contracts, CSO acquisitions, the shift from legacy primes to tech startups. Who benefits.
  • Space & Missile Defense — LEO satellite constellations, missile defense architectures, space-based ISR, and the weaponization of orbit. Arctic/Antarctic strategic positioning.

Part of the EGEX Intelligence Division. Analysis informed by INSA membership, DIA-INSA briefings, Space Force Association membership, and the 2026 National Defense Strategy. The editorial position: defense technology is too important to be covered reverently.
